Banana Bread - Diabetes Friendly

Do you have Diabetes? Does a loved-one? Are you going through a low-sugar phase?

If you answered yes to any of the above, then this scrumptious banana bread recipe is for you...

Ingredients:

  • 140g self-raising flour
  • 3 medium eggs
  • 1.2tsp baking powder
  • 200g mashed bananas
  • 4tbsp granulated sweetener
  • 40g milk chocolate chips
  • 1tsp Madagascan vanilla essence

Step-by-step instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees (160 fan)
  2. In a mixing bowl combine
    • Flour
    • Baking powder
    • Sweetener
  3. In a separate bowl, mash the bananas until smooth
  4. Whisk eggs into the bananas, adding vanilla essence to the mix
  5. Add the banana mixture to the flour mixture, whisk until smooth
  6. Mix in the milk chocolate chips
  7. Add the mixture to a loaf tin lined with baking paper and bake for 20-25 minutes
  8. Check on the bread 5 minutes from the end, as you might have to cover the top with foil to prevent burning

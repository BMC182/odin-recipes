Chocolate Chip Cookies

The unique texture of these sugar-free cookies will leave you wanting more (and more, and more...)

With only 100 calories per cookie, you can treat yourself whenever you want!

Ingredients:

  • 165g plain flour
  • 100g butter
  • 1 medium egg
  • 2tsp Madagascan vannila extract
  • 0.5tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 50g chocolate chips
  • 19tbsp granulated sweetener

Step-by-step instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 170 degrees (150 fan)
  2. Combine the butter and the granulated sweetener in a large bowl and cream together
  3. Slowly beat in the egg and vanilla extract
  4. Add the flour and bicarbonate of soda, and beat until mixture is smooth
  5. Mix in the chocolate chips and spread evenly throughout the mixture
  6. Divide evenly into 16 balls, and spread out onto a large tray lined with baking paper
  7. Flatten the balls slightly, and push in more chocolate chips on top of each ball
  8. Bake in the oven for 13-15 minutes until the cookies have a golden edge
